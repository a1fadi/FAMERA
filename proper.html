Fam-Era is a cybersecurity start-up founded by Fadi Hatu that offers affordable security software that utilises facial recognition technology to ensure you are notified of any entrants to your property when you are not there! Fam-Era ensures that you are able to monitor all parts of your house from anywhere and are always notified no matter where you are!.
